Aroon Raman

An Indian entrepreneur and famed author, Aroon's role in the genesis of DDII is phenomenal. He completed his Masters in Economics from Jawaharlal Nehru University, Delhi and his MBA from Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania. Raman currently heads a Research and Development company, and has written two books so far.

Arvind Shivakumar

Arvind is passionate about co-creative, regenerative, and sustainable lifestyles that serve all beings. To that end, he practices Systemic Constellation Methodology to process information better and ensure inclusion and love. As a co-facilitator and mentor, Arvind has helmed a self-designed course at Swaraj University dedicated to the regeneration of local cultures and economies. He is also learning Transactional Analysis Frameworks and Sociocracy to facilitate change through collaborative processes.

Binu Jacob

With a management background and experience in the corporate sales and marketing field, Binu started working in the not-for-profit sector from 2004. He started with the fundraising team of Greenpeace India by leading city operations and then taking up the national-level responsibility of various teams. He was later assigned the role of Fundraising Director at Greenpeace. Binu is a firm believer that as an individual, we all can bring the required change in society. And as CEO of DDII, his focus continues to be people-centric, along with the usage of technology and innovation to aid all endeavours.
